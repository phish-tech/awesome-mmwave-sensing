# Contributing to Awesome mmWave Sensing

👋 **Welcome! / 欢迎！**

Thank you for your interest in making this list better. This guide will help you contribute papers, tools, or datasets quickly and correctly.
感谢你愿意为这个项目做出贡献。本指南将帮助你快速、规范地提交论文、工具或数据集。

---

## 📋 Acceptance Criteria (收录标准)

To ensure the quality of this list, we follow these rules:
为了保证列表质量，请遵循以下原则：

1.  **Scope (范围)**: Must be related to **mmWave Radar** sensing (Vital Signs, HCI, Tracking, Imaging).
    * *必须是毫米波雷达感知相关（体征、交互、跟踪、成像）。*
2.  **Quality (质量)**:
    * **Papers**: Published in reputable peer-reviewed venues (ACM/IEEE conferences or journals).
    * **arXiv**: Accepted only if accompanied by **open-source code** or has high community impact.
    * *论文需发表在正规同行评审会议/期刊；arXiv 论文通常需附带开源代码。*
3.  **Format (格式)**: Please follow the table format strictly.
    * *请严格遵守表格格式。*
